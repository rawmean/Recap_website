Your privacy matters

We believe your data is yours. Here's how Recap handles your images.

No Storage

We do not store any of the images you provide to this app. Once your analysis is complete, the image is discarded.

No Sharing

We do not share any of the images you provide with any third party beyond the analysis service.

AI Processing Only

Images are shared with a generative AI model (Gemini) solely for the purpose of analyzing the scene. This is the only context in which your image is transmitted.
